Pedro Lima has proven to be one of the most active, challenging, and relevant voices in the context of contemporary national and international music.


His music seeks to explore sound universes that are unique to an eclectic background, influenced by someone who grew up listening to electronic music, hip-hop, and was part of a progressive rock band during his development. "Erudite" composition has revealed itself as a blank canvas of perfect dimensions, where a series of timbral, harmonic, and structural "investigations" have materialized. His scores manifest singular and extravagant ideas that take on different forms and expressions, depending on the context in which they wish to exist.


The music of the Portuguese composer has evolved through the contrasting projects he has created and participated in. From opera to electronic music, including community projects and various fusion experiments, it becomes redundant to try to label such a singular voice that has emancipated itself primarily through versatility. A composer who thrives in the complex socio-digital environment in which we live.
